# References for Introduction to Statistics

Curated resources to deepen your understanding of foundational statistics concepts including data, variables, populations, samples, and the distinction between parameters and statistics.

---

## Wikipedia Articles

1. [Statistics](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Statistics) - Wikipedia - Comprehensive overview of statistics as a discipline, covering its history, key concepts, and applications in science, government, and business. Excellent starting point for understanding what statisticians do.

2. [Data](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Data) - Wikipedia - Explains what data is, the difference between qualitative and quantitative data, and how data is collected and used across various fields. Helps clarify the raw material of statistical analysis.

3. [Statistical population](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Statistical_population) - Wikipedia - Defines population in statistical terms and explains the relationship between populations, samples, and inference. Essential for understanding why we sample instead of studying entire populations.

## Textbooks

4. **The Practice of Statistics** by Daren S. Starnes, Josh Tabor, and Dan Yates - W.H. Freeman (2018) - The standard AP Statistics textbook used in many high schools. Chapter 1 provides an excellent introduction to data, variables, and the statistical process with numerous real-world examples.

5. **Stats: Modeling the World** by David E. Bock, Paul F. Velleman, and Richard D. De Veaux - Pearson (2019) - Approachable introduction emphasizing statistical thinking over computation. The early chapters on data and variables use engaging contexts that resonate with high school students.

## Online Resources

6. **Khan Academy: Statistics and Probability** - Khan Academy - Free video lessons covering the basics of statistics including types of variables, sampling, and the difference between parameters and statistics. Interactive practice problems reinforce each concept.

7. **AP Statistics Course Description** - College Board - The official course framework document that defines what AP Statistics covers. Helpful for understanding the scope and expectations of the AP exam.

8. **Stat Trek: Statistics Tutorial** - Stat Trek - Clear, well-organized explanations of fundamental statistics concepts with examples and self-assessment quizzes. The sections on populations, samples, and variables are particularly helpful.

9. **Against All Odds: Inside Statistics Video Series** - Annenberg Learner - Video series that brings statistics to life through real-world applications. Episode 1 introduces the field and demonstrates how statistics is used in various professions.

10. **OpenIntro Statistics Textbook** - OpenIntro - Free, open-source introductory statistics textbook available as a PDF. Chapter 1 covers the fundamentals of data and study design with modern examples and exercises.

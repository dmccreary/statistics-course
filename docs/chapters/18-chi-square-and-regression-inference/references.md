# References for Chi-Square and Regression Inference

Curated resources to deepen your understanding of chi-square tests for categorical data and inference for regression slopes.

---

## Wikipedia Articles

1. [Chi-squared test](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Chi-squared_test) - Wikipedia - Overview of chi-squared tests including goodness-of-fit, tests for independence, and tests for homogeneity, with explanations of when each type applies and how to interpret results.

2. [Pearson's chi-squared test](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pearson%27s_chi-squared_test) - Wikipedia - Detailed coverage of Karl Pearson's original chi-squared statistic, the formula derivation, expected count calculations, and conditions required for valid inference.

3. [Chi-squared distribution](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Chi-squared_distribution) - Wikipedia - Mathematical properties of the chi-squared distribution including its relationship to the normal distribution, how degrees of freedom affect shape, and critical value interpretation.

## Textbooks

4. **The Practice of Statistics** by Starnes, Tabor, Yates, and Moore - W.H. Freeman (2018) - Comprehensive coverage of chi-square procedures and regression inference with AP-aligned examples, condition checking guidance, and practice problems matching exam format.

5. **Stats: Modeling the World** by Bock, Velleman, and De Veaux - Pearson (2019) - Accessible explanations of chi-square tests and inference for regression slopes with emphasis on interpreting computer output and communicating conclusions clearly.

## Online Resources

6. **AP Statistics Course and Exam Description** - College Board - Official guide with learning objectives for categorical data analysis and regression inference, including sample questions and scoring rubrics for free-response items.

7. **Khan Academy: Chi-Square Tests** - Khan Academy - Free video series covering goodness-of-fit tests, tests for independence, and two-way table analysis with interactive practice problems and immediate feedback.

8. **Stat Trek: Chi-Square Test** - Stat Trek - Step-by-step tutorials for calculating expected counts, computing the chi-square statistic, and finding p-values using chi-square tables.

9. **Introduction to the Practice of Statistics** - Moore, McCabe, and Craig Online Resources - Interactive demonstrations showing how sample size affects chi-square distributions and regression confidence intervals with real data examples.

10. **SticiGui: Regression** - Philip Stark, UC Berkeley - Free online chapters covering inference for regression including the LINE conditions, residual analysis, and interpreting computer output for t-tests on slopes.

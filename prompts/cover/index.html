    Generate Cover Image

    Skill

    Prompt

    Use the /book-installer cover generator guide for manual copy into a image generator

    Output

    Professional textbook cover for "Statistics" course. Place the title text "Statistics" in the center of the image. Modern educational design with a clean blue and white color scheme. Visual elements include: a bell curve/normal distribution graph, scattered data points forming a pattern, a histogram, a pie chart, and subtle mathematical symbols. Background features a montage of abstract data visualization elements. The design should be sophisticated yet approachable for high school students. No text on the image other than the title.

    Sylvia Color Themed Cover Image

    Please take the style of this image of our book mascot (Sylvia the Squirrel) and generate a new cover image for the course "Statistics".

    Technical Requirements: The image must be a wide-landscape image with a 1.91:1 width-height ratio image suitable for social media previews that are part of the open graph standard.

    Image Content: Place the title text "Statistics" in the center of the image. Place the title over the top of a montage of abstract data visualization elements. visual elements of statistics such as a bell curve/normal distribution graph, scattered data points forming a pattern, a histogram, a pie chart, and subtle mathematical symbols. The design should be sophisticated yet approachable for high school students. No text on the image other than the title.

    Modern educational design with a color theme consistent with Sylvia.
